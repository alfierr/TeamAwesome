Marble Climb

Launch your marble up the platforms and see how high you can get!
Tilt your platform to send your marble flying. Touch a platform with your marble, and you'll gain control over it!


Ways to Play


Tilt the platform using the arrow keys and press space to launch the ball!

Tilt your phone to play (in supported devices and browsers) Thrust your device upwards to launch the ball!

Use a virtual joystick to tilt the platform on other mobile devices, tap it to launch the ball!

Real-time Marble Weather

Experience marble gaming in varying conditions vaguely resembling the real-life weather in locations of your choice!

David mode

Featuring state-of-the-art "david mode" (toggleable)
